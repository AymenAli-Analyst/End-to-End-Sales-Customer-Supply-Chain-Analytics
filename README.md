🚀 RetailPulse 360
End-to-End Retail Sales, Customer & Supply Chain Analytics
📊 Project Overview

RetailPulse 360 is a professional Business Intelligence project designed to transform retail data into actionable insights.

The solution analyzes Sales, Customers, Products, Stores, Suppliers, and Supply Chain Performance through interactive Power BI dashboards, enabling executives to monitor KPIs, identify business opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.
